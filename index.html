About InfoTrix'18

A National Level Technical Symposium

InfoTrix’18 is a national level technical symposium conducted by the Information Technology department of Mahendra engineering college. Infotrix which is a great platform has both technical and fun events to present your innovative ideas and develop your technical skills. Infotrix finds the best in a person and reward them accordingly. We delightfully welcomes you all to examine yourself in different areas. The Registration fees to participate in Infotrix'18 is Rs. 200 per head.
